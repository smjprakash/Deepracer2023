About AWS DeepRacer Student
League – India 2023

AWS DeepRacer gives you an interesting and fun way to get started with reinforcement learning (RL). RL is an advanced machine learning (ML) technique that takes a very different approach to training models than other machine learning methods. The AWS DeepRacer Student league returns in 2022 with new divisions and more opportunities to win than ever before! Students can now sign in to the league to learn machine learning, train models, and compete against other students from around the globe. ​

Features of AWS DeepRacer Student League – India 2023

  1. All participants will get a global exposure in the preliminary race as they will compete in the AWS DeepRacer student.
  2. Top 100 racers will be evaluated by AWS Partners for employment opportunities in their respective organizations.
  3. All participants have an opportunity to win the AWS Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) Scholarship program, in collaboration with Intel and Udacity.
